# Roadmap

This page is for beta users. Here's a high-level look at what's coming.

<Note>
  Priorities may shift based on user feedback. No specific dates are promised.
</Note>

## Current Focus

- Stability and performance improvements
- Onboarding experience polish
- Core AI assistant reliability

## Up Next

- **Interactive nodes** — mini-apps that generate output (pricing calculators, form builders, etc.)
- **Service business nodes** — tools for coaches, consultants, and freelancers
- **Creative commerce nodes** — tools for artists, designers, and creators
- **Team collaboration** — shared workspaces and task assignment
- **Mobile app** — iOS and Android native experience

## Future

- Node marketplace — community-created and premium nodes
- Advanced integrations — Linear, Jira, Notion
- Custom workflows and automation
- API for programmatic workspace and task management
