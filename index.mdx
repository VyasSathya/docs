# Welcome to buildwhatever

buildwhatever is an AI-powered product development platform. Describe what you want to build, and the AI generates a structured plan with tracks, milestones, and specialized tools — then helps you execute step by step. Whether you're launching a coaching business, selling art online, or building a SaaS product, buildwhatever gives you the structure and guidance to ship.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Workspaces" icon="folder-open">
    Project containers with AI-generated plans, tasks, and progress tracking. Connect to a local folder for code projects or use cloud workspaces for everything else.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Assistant" icon="robot">
    A context-aware assistant that knows your project, recommends next steps, and helps you execute — from writing code to planning marketing campaigns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Nodes" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Specialized mini-apps and guides for common tasks: pricing calculators, user research frameworks, growth playbooks, and more. They appear exactly when you need them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Integrations" icon="plug">
    Connect GitHub, Slack, Sentry, email, and more. Automatically turn issues, errors, and feedback into actionable tasks.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
